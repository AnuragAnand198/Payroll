# Payroll Management System

A Payroll Management System is a software solution designed to automate, manage, and streamline employee salary processing, attendance tracking, tax calculations, and payroll reporting. This system ensures accurate, secure, and timely salary management while reducing manual errors and administrative workload.

## üìå Features

### Employee Management
- Add, update, and manage employee profiles
- Store personal, job, and bank details
- Role-based access (Admin, HR, Employee)

### Attendance & Leave Management
- Daily attendance tracking
- Leave requests and approvals
- Overtime calculation

### Salary Processing
- Automated salary calculation
- Basic pay, allowances, bonuses
- Deductions (PF, tax, insurance, penalties)

### Tax & Compliance
- Income tax calculation
- Statutory deductions
- Payslip generation with tax breakdown

### Payroll Reports
- Monthly and yearly payroll reports
- Employee-wise salary history
- Downloadable reports (PDF/Excel)

### Payslips
- Auto-generated payslips
- Employee self-service access
- Secure digital records

### Security & Access Control
- Authentication and authorization
- Encrypted sensitive data
- Audit logs for payroll actions
